To begin your training, you will need to launch your lab environment on the Red Hat Demo Platform, or RHDP. Please follow these steps to place your order.

First, log in to the RHDP portal. Once you are logged in, search for the catalog entry titled "FIXME Lab Catalog Name." When you see it in the search results, click on the entry to navigate to the catalog page. From there, click the "Order" button.

A form will appear, and you will need to fill in all the required details. Before submitting, please take a moment to review the warning at the bottom of the form. You must check the box labeled, "I confirm that I understand the above warnings" before you can proceed. Finally, click the "Order" button to officially place your lab request.

Keep in mind a few important details while you wait. First, the lab environment may take approximately "FIXME minutes" to become fully ready. You will receive an email containing your access details as soon as the environment is provisioned. Alternatively, you can retrieve your access information directly from the portal at any time.

To access your lab through the portal, navigate to the left-hand menu and click on the "Services" option. From the list that appears on the right side of the page, select your specific lab to view your access credentials.

For those needing the portal links, Red Hat associates should visit demo.redhat.com, and our partners should visit partner.demo.redhat.com.
