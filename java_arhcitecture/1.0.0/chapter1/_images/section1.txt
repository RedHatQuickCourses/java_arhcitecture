In this section, we will focus on how to effectively structure your training material. 

To ensure your content is organized and easy to follow, there are four key practices to keep in mind. First, create as many copies of this section as you need to accommodate your material. Second, be sure to remove any sections or chapter pages that are unnecessary for your specific training goal. Third, customize the remaining content so that it is relevant and appropriate for your audience. And finally, ensure that each page contains a manageable amount of information, preventing the learner from becoming overwhelmed.
